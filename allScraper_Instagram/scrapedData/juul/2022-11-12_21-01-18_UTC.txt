Juul Labs Inc has told employees on Thursday that it has stopped its bankruptcy preparations and is working on a cost-cutting program, the Wall Street Journal reported, citing company officials.⁠
⁠
The company also plans to lay off about 400 people and reduce its operating budget by 30% to 40%, the report added.⁠
